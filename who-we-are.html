WHO WE ARE

Building healthy brands
from the ground up

The Wonderful Company is a privately held $4.6 billion company committed to offering high-quality, healthy brands and helping consumers make better choices, every day.

We grow, harvest, bottle, package, and market a diverse range of products, including fruits, nuts, flowers, water, wines and juices. The Wonderful Company’s diverse holdings make it the world’s largest grower of tree nuts, America’s largest citrus grower, and the world’s largest flower delivery service with the Teleflora network of florists.

We believe there is incredible power in the Wonderful family of brands to inspire healthier choices and to support our communities. By helping nourish our neighbors with high-quality, healthy products, we believe we can grow a better world.

OUR FOUNDERS

Making the world a better place, one brand at a time

The Wonderful Company was founded by entrepreneurs Stewart and Lynda Resnick. With successful careers spanning over 50 years, they’ve always balanced achievement in business with a dedication to giving.

At the age of 19, Lynda had already
established a full-service advertising
agency

creating groundbreaking campaigns that received national attention. Around the same time, Stewart had built several lucrative businesses. Together, as a husband-and-wife team, they combined marketing savvy with financial and management acumen to pursue their shared passion for producing healthy products. They started by acquiring agricultural land in California. Over the years, they strategically expanded their company to encompass a diverse range of brands—from flowers, to nuts, to beverages and beyond.

From the very beginning, both Stewart and Lynda have made it a personal responsibility to act as good corporate citizens.

Instead of just gauging success by numbers on a spreadsheet, they measure it by how much they give back to their communities and contribute to the world.

WONDERFUL PISTACHIOS & ALMONDS

WONDERFUL PISTACHIOS & ALMONDS

From our trees to your heart

Here in the San Joaquin Valley, the warm days and cool nights work in harmony with the region’s naturally rich soils to create perfect growing conditions for the perfect snack—heart-healthy nuts loved by consumers around the globe.

Wonderful Pistachios & Almonds is the world’s largest grower and processor of almonds and pistachios that are grown, processed, and marketed under one entity, Wonderful Orchards, ensuring the finest quality every step of the way—from our trees to your heart.

Our Approach

As farmers, we believe that what you put into your body matters. The most nutritious and best-tasting foods are those that are from nature.

That’s why we are dedicated to our role as environmental stewards, investing significant time and resources on implementing water-efficient irrigation practices, biorational pest control, and reducing energy use. And we are continually evaluating how we can further lessen our impact while continuing to harvest the most delicious, nutritious snacks.

WONDERFUL CITRUS

WONDERFUL CITRUS

Committed to consistent quality

At Wonderful Citrus, we’re passionate about growing the high-quality, great-tasting citrus products you’ve come to know and love, like Wonderful® Halos® mandarins, Wonderful® Sweet Scarletts® Texas red grapefruit, lemons, limes and oranges. In fact, we are the largest integrated grower, packer, and shipper of fresh citrus in the United States. With a staff of approximately 1,500 people during peak season, nine packing facilities and state-of-the-art equipment, we supply fresh citrus to our customers year-round.

How we work

First and foremost, we’re growers. That means we control the land and have oversight over the entire process from planting to picking, from tree to table. This means you get consistent, fresh, and “wonderful” citrus—every time.

Secondly, our acreage and scale allow us to be at the forefront of progressive agricultural techniques including water conservation efforts, specialized nurseries, and integrated pest management programs.

WONDERFUL AGENCY

WONDERFUL AGENCY

Supporting our brands with creative innovation

Wonderful Agency is the in-house, full-service advertising and communications agency at The Wonderful Company. We're dedicated to creative excellence across all media (television, web, print, outdoor, POS, mobile and social media) to build consumer awareness and relevance across our brands. Wonderful Agency creates effective and innovative campaigns that match the quality of the highest echelon of global firms, while offering an environment that is far more entrepreneurial and fast-paced than a traditional agency. Wonderful Agency is highly integrated in direct brand and product development decisions with ownership, business unit leadership, legal, strategy and other key stakeholders.

How we work

Our lean organizational structure rewards people who take initiative, foresee problems, and come armed with solutions. Working within teams of media planners, producers, designers, art directors, writers, social media managers, public relations associates and technologists, our people develop agile campaigns that can be deployed within days—not months or years.

WONDERFUL REAL ESTATE

WONDERFUL REAL ESTATE

The real deal in real estate

Wonderful Real Estate acquires, develops, and manages commercial real estate properties with a particular focus on office and industrial properties in California. Our real estate development team is currently developing three business parks totaling 1,865 acres in California’s Central Valley. A number of the properties in these parks have already been purchased or leased by Fortune 500 companies and other high-quality anchor tenants. Our property management team provides a full range of professional property management services to over 30 properties owned by The Wonderful Company and its affiliates, constituting over 4 million rentable square feet. 

How we work

Our team is small, but nimble. We leverage our capabilities by utilizing in-house legal, finance, and capital projects resources of The Wonderful Company, resulting in a streamlined underwriting and deal-negotiation process. We create long-term, trusted relationships with our clients through development of the best solutions for their real estate needs, and professional property management once their project is complete.
